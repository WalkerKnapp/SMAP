Performs clustering on data using dbscan, k-means, k-medion, Gaussian mixture models or hierarchical clustering and returns positions of clusters and statistics.

gui:Parameters:
gui:N_kmeans Number of clusters
gui:N_kmeanst=N_kmeans 
gui:eps_dbscan DBSCAN: spatial neighbourhood (nm).
gui:k_dbscan Minimum number of localizations in the radius eps.
gui:k_dbscant=k_dbscan 
gui:method Algorithm for cluster analysis.
gui:posmethod How to extract centroid coordinates from clusters
gui:posmethodt=posmethod 
gui:seteps_dbscan=eps_dbscan
